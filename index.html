Tadig Restaurant

Tadig is a Persian restaurant with a warm and friendly environment located in the heart of Stockholm.
Introducing the Persian signature dishes to the world, with the best service and quality is our top goal.
Tadig is scorched rice, also known as crunchy rice, which is a thin crust of slightly browned rice at the bottom of the cooking pot. It is produced during the cooking of rice over direct heat from a flame which is included in all of our persian meals
