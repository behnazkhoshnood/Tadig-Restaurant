  • Baghali polo

    Price: 149kr(All)

    Baghali polo is a popular Iranian dish of rice, fava beans and dill. It is made by cooking rice and green broad beans in boiling water. This dish can be served in all varieties.

  • Zereshk polo ba morgh

    Price: 109kr(Chicken/ N/ L)

    Zereshk Polo ba Morgh is a Persian classic made with Zereshk (barberries), Morgh (chicken), and Polo, which is the steamed and fluffy Persian rice.

  • Khoreshte gheymeh bademjan

    Price: 109kr(Veg/ L/ N/Lamb)

    Lamb Aubergine Stew with Yellow Split Peas gives you an idea of the main ingredients of this dish.

  • Khoreshte ghorme sabzi

    Price: 109kr(Veg/ Lamb/ N)

    A stew made of herbs like parsley and coriander; cooked with kidney beans and lamb. Vegetarian ghormeh sabzi has mushrooms rather than meat.

  • Khoreste fesenjoon ba morgh

    Price: 109kr(Chicken/ N)

    This stew is flavored with pomegranate paste, ground walnuts and spices like turmeric, cinnamon, cardamom, and rosebud.
